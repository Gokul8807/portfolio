My Journey

I began with simple static pages, experimenting with background colors and text styles. The thrill of seeing instant results motivated me to dive deeper. Soon, I discovered responsive design and the challenge of making layouts work across all devices. I learned about grids, flex, and the beauty of media queries.

Then came the world of animations. I fell in love with hover effects and 3D transforms, which added life to static content. My symposium project was my first real-world challenge, where I applied these skills to build a usable product. It taught me patience, collaboration, and the value of feedback.

Now, I am refining my knowledge of accessibility, performance optimization, and modern design trends. I look forward to mastering frameworks and combining my passion for coding with my analytical mindset as a data-driven developer.
