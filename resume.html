My Web Development Journey

My journey started with curiosity about how websites function. Initially, I experimented with static pages, editing colors, fonts, and layouts. This phase was exciting because small changes brought visible results. Over time, I explored responsive design principles, learning how to make layouts adapt to different screens. I discovered the importance of media queries and fluid units. These basics formed my foundation.

Later, I focused on adding life to pages using CSS hover effects and transitions. These subtle animations made the pages interactive and engaging. The deeper I went, the more I realized the power of design in shaping user perception. During my symposium project, I applied these skills to build a real product. It was a milestone that gave me confidence to take on larger challenges.

Currently, I am expanding my skills by practicing advanced styling, improving my understanding of color psychology, and structuring layouts with readability in mind. I dedicate time to analyzing professional websites to learn best practices. My long‑term goal is to become a versatile developer capable of delivering both visually pleasing and functionally sound applications.
